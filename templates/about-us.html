Who We Are

We are a leader in comprehensive care services across the UK. Our mission is to provide exceptional and compassionate care tailored to the unique needs of each individual. We specialize in a range of services including Personal Care, Live-in Care, Respite-to-Care, Practical Support, Specialist Areas, and Palliative Care.

At Parherssia Care, we are dedicated to providing exceptional and compassionate care tailored to the unique needs of each individual. We specialize in a variety of services:

  • Personal Care - tailored elderly and in-home care.
  • Live-in Care - constant, 24-hour support at home.
  • Respite-to-Care - temporary relief for caregivers.
  • Practical Support - assistance with daily living activities.
  • Specialist Areas - focused care for specific conditions.
  • Palliative Care - compassionate end-of-life care.

Whether it's aiding daily routines or providing comprehensive live-in support, our mission is to ensure comfort and well-being for our clients and peace of mind for their families.

At Parherssia Care, we are deeply committed to enriching the lives of those in need of compassionate care. Our wide array of services spans from specialized care in dementia and Alzheimer's, to robust support for disabilities, underpinning our dedication to cater to various needs with personalized care plans. Central to our mission is the delivery of Palliative Care, offering solace and dignity to individuals and families navigating the complexities of end-of-life stages.

Upholding the highest standards of care, we are proud to be regulated by the Care Quality Commission (CQC), ensuring our practices meet stringent safety and quality guidelines. This accreditation reflects our unwavering commitment to excellence in all aspects of our service, from care delivery to professional development. Our proactive recruitment team tirelessly works to onboard the finest care professionals from the UK and abroad, striving to elevate the standard of living in care homes nationwide. In recognizing the indispensable role of carers, Parherssia Care dedicates itself to supporting our invaluable network of both paid and unpaid carers, fostering a community where everyone is acknowledged and empowered.
