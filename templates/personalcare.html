Understanding Personal Care

Personal Care services are essential for individuals who need assistance with their daily activities to maintain a quality life at home. These services cater to anyone who might find it challenging to manage personal care routines independently due to age, disability, or health conditions. Parherssia Care specializes in delivering compassionate and personalized care, ensuring the dignity, safety, and well-being of each client are upheld. Our approach improves the quality of life not just for our clients but for their families as well

Who Are Personal Care Services For?

Our Personal Care services are designed for a wide range of individuals, including the elderly, those recovering from surgeries, individuals with disabilities, and those who require assistance due to chronic health conditions. Our services are especially beneficial for those wishing to remain in their homes while receiving the necessary care to support their independence and daily living activities.

  • ✓ Individuals recovering from surgeries
  • ✓ Individuals with disabilities
  • ✓ Those requiring assistance due to chronic health conditions
  • ✓ Anyone wishing to remain in their homes while receiving support
