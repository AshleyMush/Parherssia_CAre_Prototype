Personal Care

Our professional staff offer a range of personal care services, upholding dignity and respect for our clients. These services include:

  • Regulated personal care activities, ensuring registration, inspection, and rating compliance.
  • Comprehensive personal hygiene support, including washing, bathing, and continence care.
  • Assistance with dressing, from full support to help with specific items like socks or surgical stockings.

Live-in Care

Our live-in care services are custom-tailored to each client's needs, delivered by passionate, well-trained staff ensuring independence and enjoyment at home. Key features include:

  • Personalized care plans based on in-person assessments, Person-centred support, with regular care reviews for service quality and client needs.
  • Family involvement and updates.
  • Support for appointments, mobility, and specialist needs like dementia care.
  • Thoroughly vetted, English-speaking carers with ongoing training and CQC compliance.
  • Expert post-hospital care.

Respite-to-Care

Respite care provides a short-term break, typically one to two weeks, with care from a professional different from your usual provider. These breaks, which can be planned or unplanned, are available in your own home. Our team of dedicated carers, trained to the highest standards, are ready to support your loved one, ensuring they receive the necessary care even when you're not available. As we are regulated by the Care Quality Commission (CQC), you can be confident in the safety and quality of our care. Our respite services cover a range of needs, from care for disabled individuals to companionship and condition-specific support. We offer guidance and assistance throughout the process of arranging respite care, ensuring a smooth and beneficial experience for all involved.

Practical Support

At Parherssia Care, we provide a wide range of practical help and support to people in the community who, due to extreme frailty, disability, or illness, require assistance in their own homes. Our support enables our service users to remain in their homes, close to their loved ones and local community. While in their homes, our service users can lead as normal a life as possible, maintaining dignity and surrounded by their personal belongings.
